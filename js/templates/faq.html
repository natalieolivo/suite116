Frequently Asked Questions

  • How much is security deposit fee?

    There is a $200 security deposit fee on top of total cost of rental.

  • Time for setting up?

    With any rental we give 1 hour complimentary for set up time.

  • How many people does space hold?

    The space holds 65-75 seating between the 2 adjoining rooms, or 110 standing.

  • How many bathrooms?

    There are 2 bathrooms.

  • Sound system capabilities?

    The house sound system is mpg accessible (iPod, laptop, other).

  • Can I bring my own food?

    Yes, we allow your party to bring in food and beverages.

  • Refrigeration capabilities?

    The space is equipped with a large refrigerator.

  • When do I get my deposit back?

    Deposits are given to guest at the end of contracted time, as long as space isn't damaged. FYI guest are given a 20 min grace period at the end of contracted time to leave premises.

  • Is the space wheel chair accessible?

    We do not have wheel chair access at this time.

  • Can we bring our own DJ?

    Yes. However DJs cannot bring their own subwoofers.

  • Can I stream video? Photos?

    Videos or Photos can be streamed on house TVs from a DVD format.

  • Do you have an in-house Event Designer?

    Yes. Please call us or see Family Packages for more information.

  • How much is need to save the date and time of my event?

    50% of the total rental is required to lock the date.

  • What forms of payments do you accept?

    We accepted cash, money order or credit/debit.
